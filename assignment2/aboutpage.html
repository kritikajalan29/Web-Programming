ABOUT US

SAVOIR is founded on the conviction that travel is the most powerful way to foster connection and inspire curiosity.



Founder


Kritika Jalan is from Kolkata, India. She graduated with a double degree in Computer Science and Quantitive Economics from Tufts University in 2024. Kritika's two passions have always been travelling and food. She has travelled to 48 countries so far, experiencing the unique culture, food and attractions of all these places - from experiencing to Rajasthani folk music and dance in the Thar desert to climbing snow-peaked Mount Denali in Alaska.



Kritika believes that travel should be an immersive experience where curious travellers seek culture and connection. With her belief in mind, our aim at SAVOIR is to curate these experiences and bring to light everything a place has to offer.
